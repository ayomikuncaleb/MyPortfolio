About Me

Hello! I'm Ayomikun Caleb

a passionate UI/UX Designer and Front-End Developer with a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. With 3 years of experience, I specialize in creating user-friendly and visually appealing digital experiences using Figma, Artboard Studio, and ChatGPT. My notable projects include the Roamify App, which is a travel guide platform , and Payuee, a bill payment platform.

I follow user-centered design principles and lean methodologies to ensure my designs are both beautiful and functional. My combined skills in UI/UX design and front-end development help bridge the gap between design and implementation. I approach problem-solving with a user-first mindset, conducting thorough research and continuous testing to create seamless and enjoyable experiences.

Outside of work, I enjoy listening to music, which serves as a source of relaxation and inspiration. I'm excited about future projects that challenge me to push the boundaries of design and technology. Feel free to explore my portfolio to see more of my work, and don’t hesitate to get in touch if you have a project in mind or just want to say hello!
